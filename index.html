Requirements

To ensure a fair and successful KVK, we've set up contribution targets and guidelines for everyone to follow.

Contribution Requirements

  • Power 28m-40m: 300K contribution points
  • Power 41M-50M: 700K contribution points
  • Power 51M-60m: 1.5 million contribution points (minimum 350k deaths)
  • Power 61m-70m: 2.5 million contribution points (minimum 600k deaths)
  • Power 71M-80m: 3.5 million contribution points (minimum 800k deaths)
  • Power 81M-90m: 4.5 million contribution points (minimum 1M deaths)
  • Power 91M+: 5.8 million contribution points (minimum 1.5M deaths)

How to get Points?

  • T4 Kills: x 0.15 points
  • T5 Kills: x 0.3 points
  • T4 Deaths: x 0.5 points
  • T5 Deaths: x 1 points
  • Resource Assistance: x 0.0001 points

Calculation and Fines

  • At the end of KVK, we'll record your power to calculate your final score. Flag builders and special requests will receive bonus points.
  • Please take these targets seriously, as failing to meet them will result in resource fines, matching your power with your contribution. Each point you fall short will cost 1k gold (or resources at twice the cost).
  • Remember, stricter punishments may apply for those who violate rules or refuse to contribute, including zeroing without warning or forced migration.
  • Any attempt at Stat boosting (feeding/dueling/swarming) will be analyzed by the leadership. This could result in a penalty ban from KvK rewards, or ban from MGE placement
